## Projects
 
### 🌍 [fed-em-impact-analysis](https://github.com/lucascavalcante/fed-em-impact-analysis)
**How the Fed's 2022–2024 rate hike cycle reshaped capital flows across BRICS economies**
 
A full data pipeline analyzing the impact of the most aggressive monetary tightening cycle in decades on exchange rates, capital flows, and sovereign risk across Brazil, China, India, South Africa, and Russia. Built with Python, Parquet, and Streamlit.
 
`Python` `Parquet` `Streamlit` `FRED API` `World Bank API` `IMF API`
 
---
 
### 📊 [historical-valuation-tracker](https://github.com/lucascavalcante/historical-valuation-tracker)
**Are US equities expensive or cheap — relative to their own history?**
 
A valuation dashboard covering the Magnificent 7 and select ETFs (SPY, QQQ), comparing current multiples and price levels against historical averages. Built to answer one specific question: is this asset priced high or low relative to where it has historically traded? Not a price prediction. Not a dismissal of long-term growth trends. Just context.
 
`Python` `yfinance` `FRED API` `Parquet` `Tableau Public`
 
---
 
### ⚽ [world-cup-generations](https://github.com/lucascavalcante/world-cup-generations)
**The one event every father watches with his son for the first time — twice.**
 
A data narrative built from 92 years of FIFA World Cup history, showing how the tournament keeps recruiting new generations of fans: by growing, by changing how the game is played, by minting new heroes, and by filling more stadiums than ever.
 
`Python` `Plotly` `Jupyter Notebook` `Kaggle Dataset`
